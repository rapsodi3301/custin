FAQ

Is there a multiplayer mode in Sylreth?

Yes, Sylreth supports co-op multiplayer mode. You can play with your friends! :)

What are the system requirements?

Sylreth can run on low-end PCs. Minimum requirements: Windows 10, Intel i3 or equivalent processor, 4 GB RAM, integrated graphics, 1 GB free storage space.

Which platforms can I play the game on?

Currently, Sylreth is only available for Windows PCs. We're working on Mac and Linux support, but no release date has been set yet.

How long is the game?

Currently in beta, the game takes approximately 45 minutes to complete. However, the full release will feature a much expanded experience with 8-12 hours for the main story. Completionists looking to find all secrets and abilities can expect 15-20 hours of gameplay.

How can I contact support if I encounter issues?

You can reach out to our support team via email at support@Sylreth.com. We're here to help with any issues you might face!
